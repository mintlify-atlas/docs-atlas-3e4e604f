Key Mapper is a free, open-source Android app that gives you complete control over your device's hardware buttons. Remap volume buttons, gamepad keys, keyboards, and more — then build complex macros with over 100 available actions.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Key Mapper">
    Download from [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.github.sds100.keymapper) or [F-Droid](https://f-droid.org/packages/io.github.sds100.keymapper/)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant accessibility permission">
    Key Mapper requires the Accessibility Service to detect button presses. Follow the [permissions guide](/guides/permissions) to enable it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first key map">
    Tap the **+** button on the home screen, choose a trigger (the button to remap), add an action (what happens), and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore advanced features">
    Add [constraints](/keymaps/constraints) to control when your key map activates, use [floating buttons](/features/floating-buttons) to create virtual keys, or enable [Expert mode](/features/expert-mode) for lower latency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Key Mapper is free and open-source under the GPL-3.0 license. Join the community on [Discord](http://keymapper.app/discord) for help and to share your key maps.
</Note>
